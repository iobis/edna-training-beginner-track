Water was sampled according to a standardized protocol (https://unesdoc.unesco.org/ark:/48223/pf0000384014).

Environmental DNA samples were amplified targeting the cytochrome c oxidase subunit I (COI) gene using the primer pair mlCOIintF (forward: 5′-GGWACWGGWTGAACWGTWTAYCCYCC-3′) and dgHCO2198 (reverse: 5′-TANACYTCNGGRTGNCCRAARAAYCA-3′) (Leray et al., 2013; doi:10.1186/1742-9994-10-34). Libraries were prepared in a paired-end layout and sequenced on an Illumina NovaSeq 6000 platform. For full details, see protocol: https://www.unesco.org/sites/default/files/medias/fichiers/2024/12/eDNA_expeditions_protocols_EN.pdf?hub=66910

Sequence reads have been deposited in NCBI under BioProject PRJNA1119392 (https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/bioproject/PRJNA1119392).

Sequences were processed on the PacMAN bioinformatic pipeline (https://github.com/iobis/PacMAN-pipeline).
